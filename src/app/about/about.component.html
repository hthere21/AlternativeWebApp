The Website Story

   Introducing our groundbreaking school project: a captivating website that revolutionizes the way you rate pictures from Unsplash using hand gestures! Immerse yourself in an interactive browsing experience like never before as you effortlessly navigate through an extensive collection of mesmerizing images.

   Our website harnesses the power of advanced technologies, such as computer vision and machine learning, to interpret your hand gestures and translate them into seamless rating actions. Simply swipe, pinch, or use recognizable hand movements to express your preferences for each picture in the most intuitive way imaginable.

   With an extensive library of visually stunning and diverse photographs sourced from Unsplash, our website offers an endless stream of captivating visuals to explore. No more clicking or typing - just let your hand gestures guide you through this visually rich wonderland.
